John Doe

John Doe is a fictional character often used to represent an anonymous or typical person. He has been used in legal contexts as a placeholder name in a case where the identity of the person is unknown or must be withheld for legal reasons.

John Doe is also commonly used in literature and media to depict a person whose identity is unknown or unimportant. This makes him a versatile character in many stories and scenarios.
